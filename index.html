About

From a Punjab village to the web.

I grew up in Singh Bhagwantpur, a village in Roopnagar, Punjab, and completed my 12th standard at the local Government Senior Secondary School. Technology pulled me in early — not as a subject to study, but as a way of making things people actually use.

That curiosity led me to computing training at the Sainik Institute of Management and Technology, where I started learning web development properly: structuring pages, writing clean code, and caring about how an interface feels, not just how it looks.
